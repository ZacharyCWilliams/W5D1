Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A delicious butter, bread, & cheese grilled sandwich: The quadruple decker version!

Directions

  1. Preheat skillet
  2. Butter each slice of bread
  3. Place one piece of cheese on each slice of bread
  4. Grill until cheese is melted and bread is lightly browned
  5. Enjoy!
